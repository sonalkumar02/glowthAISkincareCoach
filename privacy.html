Privacy Policy

Glowth respects your privacy. This policy explains, in simple terms, how information shared through our public website may be handled.

Information You Provide

If you contact us through a form or email, we may receive details such as your name, email address, phone number, message, and any information you choose to include.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to your questions or requests.
  • To improve our website experience and communication.
  • To maintain basic safety, reliability, and abuse prevention.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not ask for payment details on the public website.
  • We do not require sensitive personal information through the public contact page.

Cookies And Analytics

The public website may use basic browser technologies required for page functionality. If analytics are added later, they should be configured with privacy and data minimization in mind.

Data Sharing

We may use trusted service providers to host the website, receive messages, or operate support workflows. They should only process information for the purpose of providing those services.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No internet service can be guaranteed completely secure, so please avoid sending highly sensitive information through public forms.

Your Choices

You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you previously shared with us, subject to legal or operational requirements.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the public contact page.
